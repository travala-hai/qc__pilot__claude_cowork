# TC-001 — Verify homepage loads with primary search form visible

- **ID:** TC-001
- **Title:** Verify homepage loads with primary search form visible
- **URL(s) under test:** https://www.travala.com/
- **Preconditions:** Fresh browser session (new incognito/private window or cleared cookies) — no prior travala.com session. [ASSUMED: source says "no prior session expected"; incognito is the concrete way to guarantee it.]

## Steps

1. [ACTION] Open a new incognito/private browser window. [ASSUMED — concrete realization of the "fresh session" precondition.]
2. [ACTION] Navigate to `https://www.travala.com/`.
3. [ACTION] Wait until the hero section and search form are rendered (loading spinner/skeleton gone). Fallback: wait up to 15 s (KB §8).
4. [ACTION] If a cookie/consent banner or promotional modal appears, dismiss it via its close/accept control; otherwise skip. [ASSUMED — ambient obstacle, KB §7.]
5. [CHECKPOINT] Page finished loading within 10 s of navigation (source says "reasonable time"; 10 s chosen as the concrete threshold). Fail → stop and report, noting actual load time.
6. [CHECKPOINT] Hero headline is visible with non-empty text. (Exact copy is marketing-controlled and changes; assert presence, and record the text seen.) Fail → stop and report.
7. [CHECKPOINT] Primary search form is visible with exactly 4 tabs labeled **Stays**, **Flights**, **Car Rental**, **Activities** (KB §1), with **Stays** active by default. Any missing/renamed tab = fail. Fail → stop and report with screenshot.
