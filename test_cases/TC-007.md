# TC-007 — Verify selecting a destination auto-opens the date picker

- **ID:** TC-007
- **Title:** Verify selecting a destination auto-opens the date picker
- **URL(s) under test:** https://www.travala.com/
- **Preconditions:** Autocomplete dropdown visible with results for query "Hanoi" (run TC-006 steps 1–5 as setup).

## Steps

1. [ACTION] Confirm an entry with text "Hanoi, Vietnam" and badge **City** is present in the dropdown; if not present after 5 s, fail the step. [ASSUMED — element-existence guard.]
2. [ACTION] Click the "Hanoi, Vietnam" (City) entry.
3. [CHECKPOINT] Destination field value is exactly `Hanoi, Vietnam`. Fail → stop and report.
4. [CHECKPOINT] The date-picker calendar opens automatically within 2 s of the click, with **no further user action** (KB §2 cascade). Note: previously selected dates may still be highlighted from session carry-over (KB §2) — this is acceptable and should be recorded, not failed. If the calendar does not appear within 2 s, retry once by re-clicking the destination field and re-selecting; if it still does not auto-open, fail and report.
