# TC-009 — Verify selecting check-in and check-out dates

- **ID:** TC-009
- **Title:** Verify selecting check-in and check-out dates
- **URL(s) under test:** https://www.travala.com/
- **Preconditions:** Date picker open (run TC-007 as setup).
- **Test data:** ⚠️ Source dates (Sat 13 Jun 2026 → Sun 14 Jun 2026) are now in the past and unselectable (KB §2). [ASSUMED] Substitute the **first Saturday at least 30 days from the run date** as check-in and the following Sunday as check-out, preserving the source pattern (Sat→Sun, 1 night). Referenced below as `{CHECKIN}` / `{CHECKOUT}`.

## Steps

1. [ACTION] Compute `{CHECKIN}` = first Saturday ≥ 30 days from today; `{CHECKOUT}` = the next day (Sunday). [ASSUMED — date substitution above.]
2. [ACTION] If the calendar shows a month earlier than `{CHECKIN}`'s month, click the next-month arrow until that month is displayed. [ASSUMED — source assumed the calendar already showed the target month.]
3. [ACTION] If prior dates are highlighted from session carry-over, ignore them; selection below overrides. [ASSUMED — KB §2.]
4. [ACTION] Click `{CHECKIN}` in the calendar (check-in).
5. [ACTION] Click `{CHECKOUT}` in the calendar (check-out).
6. [CHECKPOINT] Check-in field displays `{CHECKIN}` in the format `D Mon YYYY` + weekday (observed format: "13 Jun 2026 Saturday"). Fail → stop and report.
7. [CHECKPOINT] Check-out field displays `{CHECKOUT}` in the same format. Fail → stop and report.
8. [CHECKPOINT] A summary string is shown matching: `{Weekday} {D} {Month} {YYYY} — {Weekday} {D} {Month} {YYYY} (1 night)` (e.g. "Saturday 13 June 2026 — Sunday 14 June 2026 (1 night)"). Fail → report, continue.
9. [CHECKPOINT] Rooms/guests picker opens automatically within 2 s of the check-out click (KB §2 cascade), showing occupancy values. Default expected: **2 Adults / 0 Children / 1 Room**; carry-over values from a prior session are acceptable — record what is shown. Picker not auto-opening = fail → stop and report.
